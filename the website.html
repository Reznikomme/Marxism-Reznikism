About Me

Welcome to my personal webpage! I am Tyson James Reznik, a proponent of Marxism-Reznikism, focusing on its application to contemporary conditions in America, especially in 2025. My philosophy aims to critique and propose solutions to modern societal issues through a Marxist lens, adjusted to the evolving challenges of our time.

Philosophy: Marxism-Reznikism in 2025

Marxism-Reznikism, an adaptation of classical Marxist thought, challenges the dominant capitalist structures, highlighting the need for systemic transformation in response to technological advancements, economic shifts, and societal transformations we see in America today.

As we enter 2025, the ongoing struggles against inequality, climate change, and the concentration of wealth demand a reevaluation of our strategies. My philosophy advocates for a radical reorganization of resources and power, empowering workers, communities, and marginalized groups to reclaim control over their lives and futures.

Through a cyberpunk lens, we must navigate a world increasingly defined by surveillance, automation, and digital capitalism. My vision seeks to bridge the gap between Marxist theory and the realities of our digital and hyper-connected age, ensuring that the revolution stays relevant to the modern world.
